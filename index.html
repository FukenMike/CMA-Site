WHO WE ARE

Chaos Management Agency (CMA) is a solo digital infrastructure and operations support company that helps small organizations simplify their technology, reduce recurring costs, and run on stable, low maintenance systems.

CMA focuses on clean setups, clear documentation, and reliable foundations so organizations are not locked into expensive hosting, bloated tool stacks, or ongoing dependency.
