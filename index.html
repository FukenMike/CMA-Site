WHO WE ARE

Chaos Management Agency (CMA) is a solo digital infrastructure and operations support company that helps small organizations simplify their technology, reduce recurring costs, and run on stable, low maintenance systems. CMA focuses on clean setups, clear documentation, and reliable foundations so organizations are not locked into expensive hosting, bloated tool stacks, or ongoing dependency.

How It Works

Audit

We review your current hosting, domain, and tool stack and identify cost and risk.

Build

We rebuild or migrate to a stable foundation with clean setup and documentation.

Handoff

You get a clear handoff so you are not dependent on ongoing support.

What We Do

Website Rebuild and Hosting Migration

Rebuild simple, fast, secure sites and move off expensive hosts. Deploy on Cloudflare Pages or Netlify with clean domain setup.

Technology Cost Reduction and Cleanup

Audit your current tools and subscriptions, remove what you do not need, and simplify the stack so fewer things break.

Digital Infrastructure Setup

Domains, DNS, email basics, forms, intake routing, and handoff documentation so your foundation is stable.

Nonprofit Website Foundations

Template based nonprofit sites with the pages that build trust: programs, contact, transparency, and policy basics.

What We Do Not Do

  • Legal advice or case guidance
  • Mental health services or clinical care
  • Marketing campaigns or social media management
  • Complex database apps or custom platforms
  • 24/7 IT management or unlimited support

Who We Help

  • Small nonprofits and community programs
  • Counselors, advocates, and program operators running services
  • Organizations paying too much for hosting, tools, or unused platforms
  • Teams that need a stable web presence without full time IT
