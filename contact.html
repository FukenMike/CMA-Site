What Happens Next

  • You email with your current setup and what you want to improve
  • We respond within 24-48 hours with clarifying questions
  • We provide a proposal with scope, timeline, and cost estimate
  • If you approve, we schedule kickoff and begin the project

What to Include in Your Email

To help us provide an accurate proposal, include the following information in your initial email:

  • Organization Overview: What does your organization do? Who do you serve?
  • Current Setup: What website platform, hosting, and tools do you currently use?
  • Monthly Costs: Approximate total monthly cost for hosting, domains, and SaaS tools
  • Pain Points: What specific problems are you trying to solve?
  • Goals: What would success look like for this project?
  • Timeline: Do you have any deadlines or target dates?
  • Budget: If you have a budget range in mind, sharing it helps us tailor our proposal

Don't worry if you don't have all this information. We can work together to gather what's needed during our initial conversation.

Response Time & Availability

Initial Response: We respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday-Friday).

Project Timeline: Most projects begin within 1-2 weeks of proposal approval, depending on current workload.

Communication: During active projects, we provide regular updates via email and schedule check-ins at key milestones.

Time Zone: CMA operates in US Eastern Time (ET), but we work with organizations across all US time zones.
