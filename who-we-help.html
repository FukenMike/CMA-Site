Who We Help

CMA serves small organizations, nonprofits, and service providers who need stable digital infrastructure without the complexity.

Our Primary Audiences

Small Nonprofits

501(c)(3) organizations and community programs operating with limited budgets and small staff. Organizations that need stable web presence and simple digital infrastructure without full-time IT support.

Community Programs

Local service providers, mutual aid networks, and grassroots organizations focused on direct service delivery rather than technology management.

Counselors & Advocates

Social workers, family counselors, case managers, and advocates running private practices or small service organizations who need professional web presence and client communication tools.

Program Operators

Directors and coordinators managing programs, services, or initiatives who need to maintain an online presence without becoming technology experts.

Common Challenges We Address

  • Paying too much for hosting, domains, or SaaS tools that are underutilized
  • Locked into expensive platforms with no clear exit strategy
  • Website built by volunteers that no one knows how to update or maintain
  • Multiple tool subscriptions doing overlapping or redundant functions
  • Technology stack so complex that staff avoids touching it
  • No documentation, no passwords, no clear ownership of digital assets
  • Previous agency or developer relationship ended with knowledge gaps
  • Need stable web presence without ongoing monthly retainers

When CMA Is a Good Fit

You're a good fit if:

You want to reduce monthly technology costs. You need a stable, documented foundation that your team can maintain. You prefer fixed-scope projects over ongoing retainers. You value simplicity and clarity over cutting-edge complexity.

You're not a good fit if:

You need custom web applications or complex interactive features. You require 24/7 support or emergency response. You want ongoing monthly management by default. You need social media management, content marketing, or advertising services.
